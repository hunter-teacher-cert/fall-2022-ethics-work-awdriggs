Gamification

Gamification is when game mechanics are applied to non-game, non-entertainment purposes. This often means adding points, badges, leaderboards, levels and quests to non-game activities.

Gamification has been applied to many different types of websites and apps. Everything from fitness trackers like Apple's Fitness Rings to apps like Chore Wars which makes a doing household chores into a game like activity.

At its best, gamification is thought to increase motivation. At its worse it can be used to manipulate people into changing their behaviors by adding incentives like points and prizes. For example, the New York Times article describes how Uber has used gamification to get drivers to drive more trips.

The purpose of this website is to experience "generic gamification." Points and badges have been added to increase your motivation to learn more about gamification. Does it work for you?
